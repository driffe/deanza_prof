Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Information Collection and Use

Professor Info Viewer extension does not collect or store any personal information from its users. The extension only processes professor names that are highlighted on web pages to display relevant information from publicly available sources.

Data Storage

All professor information is stored locally within the extension and is only used to provide information when a professor's name is highlighted. No user data or browsing history is collected or transmitted.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • Access to current webpage: To detect highlighted text and display professor information
  • Local storage: To store extension preferences and professor data locally

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the extension's support channels.
